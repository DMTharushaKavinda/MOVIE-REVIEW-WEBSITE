Our Review

Dark is a masterclass in complex storytelling, weaving together multiple timelines and generations into a coherent and mind-bending narrative. The series demands attention but rewards viewers with one of the most satisfying and well-planned conclusions in television history.

The show's atmospheric cinematography and haunting score create an oppressive, mysterious mood that perfectly complements the intricate plot. Every detail matters, and the series respects its audience's intelligence by trusting them to follow the complex web of connections.

The performances are uniformly excellent, with actors portraying the same characters across different time periods maintaining consistency in mannerisms and personality. The German setting and language add to the show's unique identity.
